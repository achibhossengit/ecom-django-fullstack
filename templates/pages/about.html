Our Story

Built from a Simple Frustration

It started when our founder couldn't find a reliable way to get everyday products delivered on time. The existing options were slow, unreliable, or just too expensive.

So we built something better. A platform where customers get fair prices, riders get fair pay, and managers have full visibility over every order from placement to doorstep.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across multiple cities and are growing every day.
